Jazz Taqi

Full Stack JavaScript Engineer

Hello. I'm Jazz, and my story begins in the winter of 2012 when I sustained an injury that took me from athletic to largely bedridden, overnight.

I struggled to come to terms with this setback until I discovered that software engineering both suited my personality, and could be done lying down. Further digging led me to learn that, despite my physical limitations, a purposeful future was possible in the inclusive world of tech. Vitalized, I started teaching myself to code.

Growing as a developer was an infinite loop of learning and building. I'd find something worth studying, consume it, apply what I'd learned to a project or coding challenge, and repeat. Early on, I was admitted to a coding boot camp called Hack Reactor, but chose not to attend. As someone who loves learning, I knew my process would eventually bear fruit...

And it did. I did eventually escape from the prison that is unemployment. I did find work as a developer!
